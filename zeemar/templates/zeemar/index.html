About Us

Zeemar Travels Services Ltd is run by cream of IATA Certified Professionals who have been piloting the Company since inception. We have been participating in the Hajj and Umrah operations since inception of the Company and have been licenced by the National Hajj Commission of Nigeria (NAHCON) on several occasions. Zeemar Travels Services Ltd is a member of the National Association of Nigeria Travel Agencies and Association of Umrah and Hajj operators of Nigeria.
